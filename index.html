About Me

My name is Francisco Fabian Flores. Born in Jersey City and raised in Elizabeth, NJ, I can appreciate the cultural diversity of our state and country and the breadth of knowledge of its people. In my youth, I practiced Tae Kwon Do and played little league baseball. I also was a volunteer tutor in my community. I have always enjoyed programming. In college, I majored in Computer Science. For a few years, post graduation, I worked as computer programmer. Then, I started a small cybercafe business, which -from a technical aspect- involved mostly but not only network administration. In an effort to learn new technologies and seek a fruitful career in software development, I recently took it upon myself to join a coding bootcamp and become a web developer. The challenging Rutgers Coding Bootcamp curriculum will surely assist in attaining this goal.
